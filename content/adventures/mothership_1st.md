## Intro

I am looking into Mothership to play, either with friends if we manage to set a date to get together for this, or as some kind of solo game.
it seems very non-mechanic, which is great to exercise our role-playing muscles, and the characters seem highly disposable (they are easy to create and the systems is not very forgiving).

in order to try it a bit, first it is time to see how the creation process works, and the amount of variability we can get from that. Without further a do, let's take a look at our crew. I could have created it via the Companion App, but it is always a pleasure rolling some dice, and it also gives me the chance to see how to structure that information in this kind of chronicles.
